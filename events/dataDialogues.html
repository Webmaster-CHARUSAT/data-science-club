Data Dialogues

The Data Science Club at CHARUSAT's Department of Information Technology organized an exclusive event, "Data Dialogues: Navigating a Successful Data Science Career," on September 10, 2023, from 10 AM to 11:30 AM. This event featured one-on-one sessions between students and seasoned data science professionals who shared their career experiences. With 114 enthusiastic participants, the event was a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and discussions about career opportunities, skill development, inspiration, projects, feedback, and improvement in the data science field.The invited experts were:

Expert:


1) Ayush Ghadiya: AI Research Consultant at Sony Research India
2) Maharshi Relia : Cloud & Network Services at Nokia Solutions & Networks Pvt. Ltd., Bengaluru
3) Megha Goriya: Incoming AI Engineer at IBM
4) Zeel Amrutiya : Solution Analyst Software Developer at Deloitte USA.


Duration:

10th September 2023 from 10am to 11:30am.


No. of Participants:

114 students
