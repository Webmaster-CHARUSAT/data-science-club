Shaping your Career

The Data Science Club at CHARUSAT's Smt. K. D. Patel Department of Information Technology organized an expert session on October 14, 2022, focused on carving out a career in data science. The event featured Mr. Rajeev Kanth, Founder of BEPEC Solutions in Bengaluru, a well-known author, educator, coach, and podcaster, as the keynote speaker.
The event covered essential topics, including an introduction to data science, career opportunities, necessary skills for data scientists, job prospects, tools and technologies for data projects, and various domains where data science plays a crucial role. Overall, it offered valuable insights into the field of data science and its career potential.

Expert:


1) Rajeev Kanth: Founder of BEPEC Solutions, educator, coach, and podcaster


Duration:

14th October 2023 from 2pm to 4pm.
